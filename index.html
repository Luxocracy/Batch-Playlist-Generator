Welcome to the Youtube Batch Playlist Generator.

Below is (or rather will be) a way of generating a playlist by matching the title to specific keywords entered below. This can also be limited to a specific youtube channel if you so wish. You may also wonder why this requires access to your YouTube account. The reason is quite simple. There wouldn't be much point, that I see, in generating a playlist without being able to save it for later viewing. Thge main reason for creating this was to be able to quickly put together a playlist from a channel series that may not have one.
